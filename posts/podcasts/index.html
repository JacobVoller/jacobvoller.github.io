Recomended Podcasts

Jacob Voller's recomended podcasts for tech and current events

Published by Jacob Voller on July 17, 2023.

Staying at the forefront of technology trends, venture capital, private equity, and mergers and acquisitions is essential for professionals in the ever-evolving tech industry. To help you stay informed and inspired, Jacob's curated list of podcasts provides valuable insights from industry experts, thought leaders, and entrepreneurs. From deep dives into emerging technologies to insider perspectives on funding and deal-making, these podcasts offer a wealth of knowledge. Below, you'll find a selection of the best episodes, offering a glimpse into the wealth of information and ideas waiting to be explored. Get ready to expand your horizons and fuel your tech journey with these captivating podcasts.

Recomended Podcasts & Shows

ALL-IN

All-in is an energetic and insightful podcast that offers a no-holds-barred exploration of current events, technology, and finance. Hosted by a dynamic group of industry experts and entrepreneurs including Chamath Palihapitiya, Jason Calacanis, David Sacks, and David Friedberg. With a mix of expertise and diverse perspectives, the hosts engage in lively debates, share valuable insights, and offer bold predictions.

Acquired

Acquired is a captivating podcast that explores the world of mergers and acquisitions, diving into the strategic decisions, competitive landscapes, and economic implications of significant business deals. Hosted by industry insiders Ben Gilbert and David Rosenthal, the podcast combines storytelling, analysis, and interviews to unravel the behind-the-scenes details of iconic acquisitions. From tech giants to legacy companies, Acquired delivers complex business concepts in an accessible and engaging manner, appealing to entrepreneurs, investors, and anyone interested in the inner workings of major corporate transactions.

The David Rubenstein Show

Through engaging interviews, Rubenstein explores the minds and experiences of influential leaders from various fields. With thought-provoking questions, David Rubenstein draws out personal anecdotes and insights, offering viewers a rare glimpse into the human side of accomplished individuals. The show inspires personal growth, emphasizing the power of conversation and knowledge sharing. Each episode leaves audiences inspired and enlightened, underscoring the profound impact of wisdom and the potential for individuals to shape the world.
